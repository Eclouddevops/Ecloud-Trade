Pattern Reference

Hammer
Small body at top, long lower wick (2x body). Appears after downtrend. Signals reversal upward.
BUY Signal
Shooting Star
Small body at bottom, long upper shadow (2x body). Appears after uptrend. Market reverses down.
SELL Signal
Three White Soldiers
Three consecutive long green candles, each closing higher than previous. Strong bullish momentum.
STRONG BUY
Three Black Crows
Three consecutive long red candles, each closing lower. Strong bearish momentum. Market goes down.
STRONG SELL
Green Marubozu
Full green body with no wicks. Buyers completely dominate. Appears in red trend = reversal up.
BUY Signal
Red Marubozu
Full red body with no wicks. Sellers completely dominate. Appears in green trend = reversal down.
SELL Signal
Bullish Engulfing
Large green candle completely engulfs previous small red candle. Strong reversal upward signal.
